# Privacy Policy for VibeQuest

Last updated: November 24, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to VibeQuest ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **No Personal Data:** VibeQuest does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
- **Local Storage:** All your preferences, favorites, and game progress are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **No Analytics:** We do not collect any analytics, tracking, or usage data.
- **No Advertisements:** We do not display advertisements or use advertising SDKs.

## How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't use, share, or process any user data.

All app functionality works entirely offline:
- Card selections
- Quiz progress
- Favorite cards
- Language preferences
- Theme settings

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## Children's Privacy

VibeQuest is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

VibeQuest does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You can simply uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: support@vibequest.app

## Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- Google Play Store requirements
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

---

**Summary:** VibeQuest respects your privacy by not collecting any personal data. All functionality works offline and all data stays on your device.
