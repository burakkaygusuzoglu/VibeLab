## Introduction

VibeQuest ("we," "our," or "us") operates the VibeQuest mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy and describes how we handle information when you use our App.

## Our Privacy Commitment

**WE DO NOT COLLECT ANY PERSONAL DATA.**

VibeQuest is designed with privacy as a core principle. The App operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location data (GPS, IP address)
- ❌ Device information (device ID, advertising ID)
- ❌ Usage analytics or statistics
- ❌ Crash reports or diagnostics
- ❌ Cookies or tracking technologies
- ❌ Photos, contacts, or other device data
- ❌ In-app behavior or preferences (beyond local storage)

## Local Data Storage

The App uses **local storage only** (iOS UserDefaults, Android SharedPreferences, AsyncStorage) to save:

- Your app preferences (theme, language settings)
- Favorited cards
- Game progress and scores
- Onboarding completion status

**This data:**
- ✅ Stays on your device only
- ✅ Is never transmitted to any server
- ✅ Is deleted when you uninstall the App
- ✅ Is not accessible to us or any third party

## Third-Party Services

We do **NOT** use any third-party services including:

- No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No social media SDKs
- No crash reporting tools
- No customer support platforms
- No cloud storage or backup services

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

VibeQuest is rated 12+ due to infrequent/mild mature/suggestive themes. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, our App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## GDPR Compliance (European Users)

Since we collect no personal data, GDPR data subject rights do not apply. However, for transparency:

- **Right to Access:** There is no data to access
- **Right to Rectification:** There is no data to rectify
- **Right to Erasure:** Uninstalling the App removes all local data
- **Right to Data Portability:** There is no data to export
- **Right to Object:** There is no data processing to object to

## CCPA Compliance (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), we declare:

- We do not sell personal information
- We do not share personal information with third parties
- We do not collect personal information

## Permissions

VibeQuest requests **NO permissions** on your device:

- ✅ No camera access
- ✅ No microphone access
- ✅ No photo library access
- ✅ No location services
- ✅ No contacts access
- ✅ No network access (App works fully offline)

*Note: The App may request notification permissions for optional daily challenge reminders. This is entirely optional and can be disabled in device settings.*

## Audio Files

The App includes optional sound effects that are:
- Stored locally within the app bundle
- Played only on your device
- Not recorded, transmitted, or analyzed in any way

## Data Security

Since no data leaves your device:

- Your information cannot be intercepted
- Your information cannot be breached
- Your information cannot be sold or shared
- Your data is as secure as your device

We recommend:

- Download apps only from official stores (App Store, Google Play)
- Keep your device OS up to date

Not applicable. No data leaves your device.
